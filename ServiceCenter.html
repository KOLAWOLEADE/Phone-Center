WARRANTY AND POLICIES

From the date of purchase, different OEMs’ comes with different warranty period and policies. Highlighted are few brands warranty periods.

Apple: Any device that develops any manufacturer’s defaults during the period of the 12 month warranty periods gets an instant swap as Apple does not fix warranty devices (device only).

Samsung: Samsung offers 2 years warranty but only swap devices within 1 week (7 days) of purchase if any manufacturers fault is detected.

TECNO, INFINIX: They offer 13 months warranty on all purchased devices. The service center (CARLCARE) swaps devices within 48 hours (2 days) of purchase. Also they swap a device when it return rate is 4 at the service center.
