Pay on Delivery

At Zubek, we strive to make shopping online as easy and convenient as possible. One way we do this is by giving our buyer community the choices they’ve asked for. When you shop on Zubek, you can choose to pay securely in advance with internet banking, with your ATM/Debit card, or on delivery with cash or POS.

  • If you choose to pay in advance with internet banking or your ATM/Debit card, Zubek can protect you 100% with our Buyer Protection Program. When you’re covered by our Buyer Protection program, you can shop with complete confidence and ultimate peace of mind: if something goes wrong, we help make it right with a refund, repair, or replacement.
  • You can also choose to Pay on Delivery in Lagos, and many other States across Nigeria. You simply pay the courier with cash or with your ATM/Debit card when your order is delivered.
